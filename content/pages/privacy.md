## Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

At **Visual Sanctuary**, accessible from visualsanctuary.com, the privacy of our visitors is one of our main priorities. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Visual Sanctuary and how we use it.

### 1. Information We Collect

- **Log Files**: Like many other websites, we use log files. These files log visitors when they visit the site. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
- **Analytics**: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how our visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve the user experience. GA4 may use cookies to collect anonymous usage data.

### 2. Cookies and Web Beacons

Visual Sanctuary uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

### 3. Third Party Privacy Policies

Our Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information.

### 4. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.
